Transportation Engineering Discipline was introduced in the Department of Civil Engineering, National Institute of Technology, Warangal in the year 1968 under the aegis of UNESCO-UNDP and Government of India, in realization of the emerging area and the urgent need to train Engineers/Planners in this discipline. This is the first Institution in India to have started a full-fledged M.Tech Degree Programme in Transportation Engineering. Under the able guidance of Prof. Martin Ekse of Washington State University, USA, Prof. V.V. Sylyanov of Moscow Automobile and Road Construction Institute, USSR, and other distinguished experts in India. The Transportation Engineering discipline has not only emerged as a forerunner in the country, but also created competent Transportation Engineering cadre in the country, who are now serving in various National and International bodies.
